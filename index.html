About Me

My name is Erica Bennett, and I am a current graduate student in the Religion in Culture Masters at the University of Alabama. This is a site for me to showcase the work I am currently doing in my program but also other media I get to work on outside of school. My hopes for this page is to create a well-designed and beautiful site for not only potential employers or collaborators to easily access my work, but also a place to easily show my friends and family what I have learned and created in the last few years.

Podcasting

Part of my interest in Digital Humanities focuses on how academics can better educate outside their normal circles. Meaning, how can smart people get information out to a wider audience. For me, I have found that podcasting is an excellent medium for this. My goal as a podcaster is to create academic podcast that feel like nonacademic podcasts. By that I mean, lots of times academic-focused podcast tend to feel like lectures over microphones. I love podcasts and want people to not only love their fantasy, reality, or lifestyle podcasts, but also enjoy and consistently listen to an educational podcast network.
